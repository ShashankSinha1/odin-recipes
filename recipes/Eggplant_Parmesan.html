Description:

The whole family will love this classic eggplant parmesan! Slices of tender eggplant coated in homemade breadcrumbs, and then layered with melted cheeses & rich marinara! Whether you'd like to bake or fry the eggplant, this recipe has a method for both.

Ingredients:

  • Marinara sauce
  • Eggplant
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Herbs
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic powder

Steps:

  1. Slice the eggpland and sweat it
  2. Mix your breadcrumbs with your herbs and garlic powder and some parmesan cheese
  3. Bread the eggpland
  4. Pan-fry the eggpland for 2-3 minutes on each side
  5. Assemble your eggpland with the marinara and top it off with parmesan
  6. Bake this at 400 degrees for 30 minutes and enjoy!
