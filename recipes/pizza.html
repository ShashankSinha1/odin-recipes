Description:

Homemade cheese pizza crust has a delicious flavor and texture that only comes from YOU!

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Mozzerella cheese
  • Oil
  • Tomato sauce
  • Italian seasoning

Steps:

  1. Flatten out your pizza dough
  2. Spread oil over the dough
  3. Top the dough with the pizza sauce, then the sliced mozzarella, the shredded mozzarella, and grated parmesan. Sprinkle lightly with Italian seasoning.
  4. Bake at 475 degrees for 20 minutes or until the crust is golden brown then enjoy!
